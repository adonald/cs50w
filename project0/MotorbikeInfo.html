About Me

Welcome to my little website. Here I am slowly compiling a collection of projects I have created. Some fix problems I have encountered, others I just enjoyed coding! I am currently studying for a BSc in "Computing and IT with Applied Mathematics" at the Open University, so hopefully these projects will get more complex as my learning continues.

My day job consists of working on the project delivery team for a company that creates recipe software. It is a small company, so I get a chance to get my teeth into a variety of projects on top of my core role. In my spare time, I create websites for friends, administer a couple of Linux servers, and generally tinker about with computers. The rest of my time I spend either riding my motorbike, raising money for local causes with the Round Table, or looking after my young, growing family :-)
